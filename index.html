About

I am currently a Computer Science Graduate Student at Rutgers University, before which I worked at Barclays, India for two years. I am deeply passionate about understanding and developing distributed systems. Distinct individual systems working together to provide a seamless service is what excites me. I am interested in the fields of Distributed Systems, Cloud Computing, and the infrastructure Systems at large.

You will always find me working on some idea, anywhere from an android app to setting up a mini compute cluster for my roommate studying Data Science. Rest of the times badminton and deep conversations with people fill up my day.
